This is the start of a small notebook for the Sigilweaver suite, the
collection of open-source readers, stores, and reconstruction tools
that live across the
[Sigilweaver GitHub organization](https://github.com/Sigilweaver).

Expect short writeups about:

- Reverse-engineering closed binary formats (Thermo `.raw`, Bruker `.d`,
  Waters `.raw`, Qlik QVD, Alteryx YXDB) on public corpora.
- Why Lance is a surprisingly good fit for mass spec, variants, and
  k-space data.
- Stability and performance work on the OpenProteo stack.
- Reconstruction notes from OpenKSpace (SENSE, GRAPPA, CS).
- Release notes for every public version bump.
